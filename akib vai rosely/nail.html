Nail care and shapes tips

Best nail care advices

There are various types of nail shapes, and it’s an advantage of having an idea about best nail shape that suits your personality, and enhance attractiveness of your fingers.
Now days, designs and trends of nails are influenced by famous celebrity styles. Here in this article, we will discuss different nail shapes, and it will help you to choose ideal one for you.
ALMOND SHAPE: Almond shape nails have a longer, and softer tip which looks like a rounded shape. This shape reduces chubby look, and makes the look of your hands more delicate.
To get this shape, simply leave the nails long, and then file the sides only to  get an  almond shape. But it’s difficult to maintain that shape that’s why almond manicures strengthened with acrylic or gel.
OVAL SHAPE: Oval nails are different from round ones. Oval shapes are usually recommended to people having wide, and narrow nail beds. It gives an elegant look to your nails just like the almond shape.
 SQUARE SHAPE: Although the trend of square shape nails is outdated but, still in demand. Short length square shape nails look more stylish. You must have to be remembered and careful that this shape will only enhance the beauty of thin and long fingers but, makes thick fingers bigger.
It looks good on long and medium nails but, its edges need an extra care as they can easily break. That’s why it is usually recommended to those having strong nails.


 MOUNTAIN PEAK SHAPE: Having mountain peak nails is a good and bold choice, and give extraordinary great look to your personality. The shape is sharper as compared to almond.
 ROUNDED SHAPE: Round shape is a correct choice for women having short fingers. It lengthens the short fingers and makes the nail bed visually wider. Usually those girls choose this shape who don’t want any nail interference with washing-up or working. The round and smooth edges of this shape cannot be chipped easily and makes your nail stronger.
 SHORT NAILS: The short nails are comfortable, as well as fashionable. Having this kind of shape makes it very convenient to use your computer or smart phones.
 EDGE NAILS: Now days, edge nails are one of the trending nail shapes. These type of nails are pointed but less destructive. An edge like shape makes your fingers longer and thinner.
NAIL CARE TIPS:It’s not only the nail shape and nail design that makes your personality graceful but, taking care of your nails also plays a vital role because, it can frame your manicure.
Once you have decided the nail shape that will suit your personality, then you must have to know the proper way of filing the nails because, the shape of your nails makes your manicure elegant and beautiful. 
Here are few tips:
 CHOOSE CORRECT NAIL FILE: Before shaping the nails, you must have the right tools. Nail files made from emery boards are the best choice. Do not use metal nail files because they can cause chipping and peeling. These emery board nail filers not only protect your nails from getting weak, but they are cheap too.
 CLIP YOUR NAILS: Only longer nails can cause breakage and clipping. It’s very uncomfortable when your nails get caught on something or when your nails snag. Use sharp and clean pairs of clippers while trimming your nails.
 FILING FROM ONE DIRECTION: File your nails from only one direction. Many people file their nails from both sides in back and forth position, it is not good because, it causes the nails to break and chip. Do not file deeply your nail edges as it can weaken the nail wall.
ADDITIONAL NAIL CARE TIPS:
Nail polish gives protection to your nails while filing. If old nail polish left on your nails,file first before removing it. Only file your nails when they are dry to avoid getting breaks and chips. Buff your nails after filing so, they become shiny and smoothy.Only choose the shape that suits your fingers and hand because, when it comes to nail care, you should always focus on health over length.Try to wash your hand with good quality water because, poor water can make your nails fragile and weak.Avoid finger nail-biting.
Thus, nail care is considered as the proper maintenance of your nails. It is important because it helps to prevent nail problems, such as fungal infections. Now, it’s up to you that how to keep care of your nails
