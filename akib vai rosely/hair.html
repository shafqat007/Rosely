How to stop men hair loss and regrow naturally

Best solution for hair fall

Hair is considered as the most noticeable part because, it enhances our personality, and beauty.But now days, hair loss has become a very severe problem to every women and men of all age types. 
For women, it is one of the most frustrating symptoms as it is the only way of expressing personality, and style. But most of the causes of hair loss can be treated or stopped, luckily. Let us discuss some major causes, treatments, and tips for hair loss in this article.
SOME MAJOR CAUSES:The causes of hair loss can be categorized into following:
• DEFICIENCIES: Vitamin B7, VITAMIN B12, Zinc, Silica, Iron & essential  fatty acids.
• HORMONAL IMBALANCES: High testosterone, estrogen dominance, thyroid imbalance, high  cortisol, insulin resistance, PCOS, giving birth, Menopause.
• ENVIRONMENTAL CAUSES: Metal toxicity, chemical treatments, mites, pollution.
• HAIR DISORDERS: Alopecia areata, Androgenetic alopecia, Cicatricial (scarring) alopecia,  Central centrifugal cicatricial (scarring) alopecia.
• DIET AND POOR NUTRITION:  Weight loss, excessive vitamin A, eating disorder, protein, and  iron deprivation.
• MEDICATIONS: Birth control pills, high dose vitamin A, blood thinners, anabolic steroids,  medicines that treat depression, arthritis, high blood pressure, heart problems, and gout.


HOW DERMATOLOGIST TREATS HAIR LOSS?
There is huge variety of treatments for hair loss. Dermatologist recommends earlier treatment, as it become more difficult to handle severe hair loss.
Some treatment plans are following:
• MINOXIDIL: This medicine is directly applied on the scalp, and stimulate the growth of hairs. The drug is approved by food and drug administration (FDA), and it is the only hair re-growth  medicine. 
The drug comes in the form of foam or gel, that is applied on your scalp. Treatment  takes at least six months for proper hair growth. Some possible side effects are unwanted hair  growth on the skin of hands, and face, scalp irritation, and rapid heart rate.
• LASER DEVICES: Laser light emitting devices such as combs, and brushes might stimulate the  growth of hairs.Because these devices have been classified as medical devices by FDA so, they do  not undergo any vigorous testing.
• CORTICOSTEROIDS: Sometimes body inflammation may cause hair loss. For this,dermatologist   may give corticosteroid injection into your scalp. This is the best way to stop inflammation that  usually occurs when an individual is suffering from Alopecia areata.
• FINASTERIDE: The drug is FDA approved, and used to treat hair loss in men. Many men  experience slowness of hair loss while taking this medicine. The drug works by stopping the  production of Dihydrotestosterone (DHT), a male hormone.Side effects include increased risk of  prostate cancer, and diminished sexual function, and sex drive.
HAIR TRANSPLANT THERAPIES: 
If you are not getting better results from other treatments, then you might think about hair transplantation to improve your hair density.
Some common hair transplantation therapies are: 
• BIO-GEN THERAPY: Dominant hair follicles are activated by this therapy to initiate the growth   of hairs. All essential nutrients are delivered by this therapy to the roots via micro injections. Overall procedure is painless, and takes only 30 minutes.
• MESOTHERAPY: In this type of therapy injections of amino acids, minerals,and vitamins are  injected into mesodermal layer of skin to enhance hair growth.
• PLATELET RICH PLASMA THERAPY(PRP): In PRP, essential growth factors are extracted  from your own blood, concentrated, and then injected directly into the scalp to enhance the  process  of hair growth.
• HAIR ROOT STIMULATION TREATMENT (HRST): This therapy enhances cycle of hair  growth, and stimulate blood circulation. Root must have to be engorged with blood as it is the most   important part of hair growth.
PREVENTION AND MANAGEMENT TIPS:
There are many tips, and home remedies that can prevent hair loss. Few of them are:
 Take a balanced diet in order to get an adequate amount of protein and iron. Avoid tight hairstyles like braids, buns, and pony tails. Avoid hair instruments like iron straightener, and curler. Do not brush wet hairs.
NATURAL REMEDIES:• MASSAGING: Massage your scalp with oil to enhance hair growth because massaging activates  dormant follicles of scalp.
• ALOE VERA: Aloe Vera gel conditions your strands, and soothes your scalp. It releases blocked  hair follicles, and reduces dandruff.
• COCONUT OIL: Essential fatty acids present in coconut oil penetrates deeply inside the shaft,  and reduce protein loss.
• FISH OIL: Omega fatty acids present in the fish oil are very helpful in improving your strands  from inside.
• ONION JUICE: Onion juice is the best choice to treat hair loss. It improves circulation, and   stimulate hair growth. 
• LEMON: Lemon oil maintains your scalp healthy, and encourages the growth of your strands. If you want to make your hair thick, and healthy, follow the above tips, and home remedies. You   will get better results in few months.
